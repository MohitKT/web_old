Biography

Mohit Kumar is serving as Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) at National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur (H.P.), India. He received his Ph.D. in 2018 from IIT Guwahati under guidance of Prof. Pradip K Das. He has completed his M. Tech. in 2013 (GATE Scholarship) in IT with specialization in Intelligent Systems from IIIT Allahabad. He has published several research papers in reputed international conferences journals. His current research areas of interest include Speech Processing, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence & IoT.
